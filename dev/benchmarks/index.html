Benchmarks

The benchmarks below were run on Julia 1.8.0.

Default example

X = [40 30 20 10; 35 50 100 75; 30 80 70 120; 20 30 40 50]
u = [150, 300, 400, 150]
v = [200, 300, 400, 100]
@benchmark ipf(X, [u, v])
